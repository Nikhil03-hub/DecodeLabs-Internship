FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Still have questions? Say hello — we reply fast.

What makes Nexus AI different from other productivity tools?

Nexus AI is purpose-built around intelligent automation. Unlike tools that require manual process management, Nexus learns from your team's patterns and proactively suggests optimizations. Our AI layer sits across your entire workflow — not isolated in individual features — creating a system that continuously improves.

Can I migrate my data from other platforms?

Yes! We offer one-click migration from Notion, Asana, Monday.com, Trello, Jira, and 50+ other platforms. Your data, structure, and attachments transfer completely. Most migrations complete in under 15 minutes, and our team provides hands-on support for enterprise migrations.

Is my data secure and private?

Security is our absolute priority. We are SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and ISO 27001 certified. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never train our AI on your private data without explicit written consent. Enterprise plans include dedicated infrastructure options.

What integrations does Nexus AI support?

We support 200+ native integrations including Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Figma, Linear, and many more. Our open REST API and webhook system lets you connect to virtually any tool in your stack.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel anytime from your account settings in one click. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation, giving you plenty of time to export everything. We believe you should stay because Nexus is genuinely valuable — not because leaving is difficult.
